Floating Mute is a convenient app that lets you quickly mute videos with a single tap. Whether you're watching a YouTube short, a Reel, or any other video, Floating Mute gives you instant control over the audio.

No more fumbling for the volume buttons or navigating through menus - with Floating Mute, you can easily switch between mute and unmute with just one tap, allowing you to focus on the visuals or listen to the audio as needed.

Perfect for when you need to watch a video in a quiet space, or when you just want to focus on the visuals, Floating Mute is a simple yet powerful tool that makes video watching easier and more convenient.
